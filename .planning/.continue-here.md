- JobForge = Workforce Intelligence (WiQ) Platform = the data model
- JobForge "feeds" JD Builder Lite (and will feed other HR artifact modules)
- Other planned modules: SOMC Creator, Job Notice Creator, Learning Plan, Performance Agreement

**v3.0 Scope:**
See `.planning/v3.0-REQUIREMENTS.md` for full requirements including:

**Data Architecture:**
- Medallion pattern: Bronze → Silver → Gold
- JD Builder consumes JobForge Gold model
- Full provenance chain from OaSIS through JobForge to JD output
