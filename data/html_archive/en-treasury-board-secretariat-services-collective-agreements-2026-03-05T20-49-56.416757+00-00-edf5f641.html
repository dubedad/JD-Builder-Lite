Occupational groups for the public service

The following table (called the table of concordance) links occupational groups, sub-groups and job evaluation standards. Each row lets you access a group’s definition, its sub-group definitions (when applicable), its job evaluation and qualification standards, collective bargaining information and rates of pay.

Tip

To find information quickly, you can use the “Filter items” box below and to the right. Enter the group or sub-group abbreviation preceded by an open-ended parenthesis. For example:

  • (AI - for the Air Traffic Control group
  • (CR - for the Clerical and Regulatory classification
  • (EN-ENG - For the Engineering sub-group
